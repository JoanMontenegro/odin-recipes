How to make a delicious Bolognesa

What you need for this recipe :

  • 500 grams of pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 big pot
  • 1 kilo of grinded meat
  • 1 kilo of tomate

How to prepare the bolognesa

  1. Add water to the pot, 1 liter should be enough for 500 grams of pasta
  2. Add one tablespoon of olive oil to the water
  3. Add some salt to the water
  4. put the pasta in the pot
  5. let it boil for 5 minutes

After the five minutes you have your pasta ready. now we need to make the bolognesa sauce
